How to attend

Register for MFF

The concert is open to all attendees of the convention. Badge price at the door is $40 for a Friday badge, or $90 for the full weekend badge.

Reg line can be long

Pick up your badge Thursday evening or Friday morning to make sure you can attend the concert.

MFF Registration Info

Getting there

  • Public transit: take the CTA Blue Line to the Rosemont stop, then transfer to the Rosemont Circulator bus which will bring you to the convention center.
  • Public parking: Parkway Bank Parking Garage is $15 a day.
  • Badge pickup: Hall A in the Donald E. Stephens Convention Center.
    • Concert: 2:30pm - 3:30pm on Friday Dec. 6th, Grand Ballroom (Main Stage) in the Hyatt Regency O'Hare.
