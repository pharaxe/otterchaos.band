How to attend

Register for MFF

The concert is open to all attendees of the convention. Badge price at door is $40 for a Friday badge, or $90 for a weekend badge.

Reg line can be long

Pick up your badge Thursday evening or Friday morning to make sure you can attend the concert.

Getting there

  • Public transit: the Rosemont Circulator bus can bring you from the CTA Rosemont Blue Line stop to the convention center.
  • Public parking: Parkway Bank Parking Garage is $15 a day.
  • Badge pickup: Hall A in the Donald E. Stephens Convention Center.
